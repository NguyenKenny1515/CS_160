The Objective

The purpose of this web application is to develop an online recreation system for a smart city, which will help citizens choose various activities for them or their children. Their choice will be made based on various criteria, such as age (for example preschool, grades 9-12, adults, seniors), type of activity (for example sports, arts), proximity to the venue (for example at a distance of up to 5 miles), its cost, dates and time, etc.

What You'll Need

  1. An Account
  2. A Credit Card
  3. 10 Minutes


How It Works

The platform will allow users to purchase points (Robucks) for the activity they are interested in registering for with the use of their credit card. This purchase of points will act as an electronic wallet that will fund the events for which the person wishes to sign up for.

Events are mapped geologically with Google Maps. First the user must press the "Find Events" button at the top to populate all events on the map. Once populated, click on any of the markers to display the details.




Once you find an event that interests you, click on the event listed below to register for it. You'll receive a confirmation message.




If the user doesn't feel like creating an account, that's completely fine as well! Those who want to simply browse events but not register may click the 'Explore Events' tab in the navigation bar.
